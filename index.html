About

My name is Sanja Božović. I was born in a small town called Gornji Milanovac.

Even though photography is only a hobby of mine, it is something I'm very passionate about.

I love doing portrait photography because it really allows me to capture emotions.

I'm self-taught with a sincere passion for all things photographic, sincere people, and beautiful spaces.

When I’m not doing all things photo-related, I’m probably playing guitar.

I just love expressing my creativity through my camera and guitar.
